The Beginning

A Vision Takes Root

"In the heart of Rajasthan's blue city, where ancient woodworking traditions have flourished for centuries, a new chapter in craftsmanship began to unfold."

In the year 1999, amidst the sun-drenched landscapes of Jodhpur, India—a city renowned the world over for its extraordinary furniture-making heritage—Mr. Arvind Sankhla laid the foundation of what would become a beacon of excellence in the global furniture industry. With an unwavering commitment to quality and an astute vision for international markets, Artisan Alliance was born as a modest wooden furniture manufacturing and trading enterprise.

The turning point arrived in May 2001, when Artisan Alliance embarked upon its first direct export venture—a moment that marked the beginning of an extraordinary transformation. What started as a humble endeavour would soon evolve into a fully export-oriented powerhouse, carrying the essence of Indian craftsmanship to discerning clientele across the globe.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

1999

The foundation of Artisan Alliance is laid in Jodhpur by Mr. Arvind Sankhla, establishing a commitment to exceptional wooden furniture craftsmanship.

May 2001

The pivotal moment arrives—Artisan Alliance dispatches its first direct export shipment, marking the transition to a global enterprise.

2001-2010

A decade of remarkable expansion. The company diversifies into metal furniture, houseware, storage solutions, and bath accessories, building partnerships across continents.

Today

A thriving enterprise with 300+ skilled artisans, serving 100+ customers across 27 countries, dispatching 45+ high-cube containers monthly.

Our Expertise

Mastering the Art of Fine Living

At Artisan Alliance, we do not merely manufacture furniture—we curate experiences. Our master craftsmen, many of whom represent generations of woodworking tradition, bring to life an exquisite array of products that grace homes, hotels, and commercial spaces across the world.

From the warmth of handcrafted wooden furniture to the contemporary elegance of metal designs, from refined houseware and tabletop treasures to innovative storage solutions, from thoughtfully designed pet feeders to luxurious bath furniture and accessories—each piece bears the unmistakable signature of our dedication to excellence.

Our Infrastructure

Where Vision Meets Capacity

Nestled within Jodhpur's renowned furniture district, our expansive manufacturing and finishing facilities stand as testament to our commitment to scale and precision. With significant covered production and finishing space, we have created an environment where traditional craftsmanship harmonises with modern manufacturing excellence.

Our greatest asset, however, remains our people. A dedicated workforce of over 300 skilled artisans and professionals pour their expertise and passion into every piece that bears the Artisan Alliance name. Their collective mastery ensures unwavering quality control and dependable delivery schedules that our global partners have come to rely upon.

Our Philosophy
